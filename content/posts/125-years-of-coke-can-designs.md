Today’s post is an article that intrigued me from [Adfreak](http://www.adweek.com/), one of my favorite “other” advertising blogs. I actually wrote about a much older post about who creates these interesting Coke designs [here](http://www.insidethatad.net/2010/09/designs-on-back-of-coke-cans.html). But as you can see, there are a number of ad agencies working on designs for Coka-Cola’s flagship product.
The designs are being created in preparation for Coca-Cola's 125th anniversary celebration.
