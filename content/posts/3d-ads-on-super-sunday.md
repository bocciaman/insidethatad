[Dreamworks Animation](http://www.dreamworksanimation.com) and PepsiCo's [SoBe Lifewater](http://www.thrillicious.com) have joined together with [Intel Corporation](http://www.intel.com) and [NBC](http://www.nbc.com) to present an ad in 3-D for the first time in television history.  The ad will be debut trailer for Dreamworks’ new 3-D animated film "[Monsters vs. Aliens](http://www.apple.com/trailers/dreamworks/monstersvsaliens/)," which comes to theaters in the U.S. on March 27th. To get the full experience of the trailer it must be viewed using 3-D glasses that the [Intel Corporation ](http://www.intel.com)has produced. The glasses will be distributed by PepsiCo’s [Sobe Lifewater](http://www.thrillicious.com/) brand.
