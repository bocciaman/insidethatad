The Internet is a medium where people generally feel a lot less restricted when it comes to expressing their true feelings. Why, because most of the time the opinions people post are anonymous. The most recent victim of the general public's  anonymous opinions is Amazon.co.uk's product page for "Bic for Her" pens. The pens—which are described right there in the technical specs as being "designed to fit comfortably in a woman's hand" with an "attractive barrel design available in pink and purple.
This technical description got a wide range of comical comments. Read some of them on on [Bics' UK product page](http://www.amazon.co.uk/product-reviews/B004FTGJUW).
