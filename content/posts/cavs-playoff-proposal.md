I thought the spot above was extremely funny. It a parody on the Heineken commercial created by [TBWA\NEBOKO](http://insidethatad.blogspot.com/www.neboko.nl) in the Netherlands. Except in the Cleveland Cavaliers commercial they are screaming about shoes while the ladies in the other room are going wild about an engagement. But to see a pro sports team making fun of themselves, that's not something you see everyday.
