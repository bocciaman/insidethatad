Apparently April 3rd is national cleavage day in [New Zealand](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Zealand). I wouldn’t have found out about it if it wasn’t for one of my favorite ad blogs, [Adfreak](http://adweek.blogs.com/adfreak/2009/04/national-cleavage-day-gets-global-support.html). According to the post it’s a day centered around bras and other partially scandalous clothing. The day was made popular via the popular social network [Twitter](http://twitter.com/). Much like the Adfreak blogger I too think the U.S. must hurry up and come up with a similar day. We can have a day where we all wear a special article of clothing. But I don't think it needs to be anything that will offend anyone.
