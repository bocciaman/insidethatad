The ad above is a commercial on behalf of the [Special Olympics](http://www.r-word.org/r-word-not-acceptable-psa.aspx). The campaign is aimed to educate and stop the spread of the “R” word, it being the derogatory term “retarded.” It aims to educate people that the R-word is just as derogatory as any word out there.

I believe the commercial was created by BBDO, New York.
