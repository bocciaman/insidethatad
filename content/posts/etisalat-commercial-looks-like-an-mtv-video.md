My wonderful wife turned me on to this ad for Etisalat. Etisalat is a multinational Emirati based telecommunications services provider, currently operating in 15 countries across Asia, the Middle East, and Africa. I wanted to discuss the ad here on Inside That Ad because I believe it's my first time seeing an ad that I confused with a music video. You don't find out that it is actually advertising a product until the end of the commercial. All the scenes are not in any sort of sequence, there is a slogan and everyone in the commercial is dancing in some fashion.

### Commercial Description
The commercial also features a fight scene between the two stars Ramadan and Van Damme that was made to look like a scene from one of Van Damme's really old but good movies - [Blood Sport](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0092675/), I watched at least three times. Another interesting observation about this commercial is its duration lasting 2 minutes and 33 seconds. The spot is definitely on the longer side of anything I've seen. In the US, commercials of this length usually only appear during advertising's big day - the Superbowl. Not sure if the big game is seen in Egypt, it is, it's most likely recorded and aird later, right?
