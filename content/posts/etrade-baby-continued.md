E*Trade will be bringing back the talking baby for this year's Superbowl. The baby will have a new spot that the company is keeping on the DL. This year the talking baby has even more of of a following and is marketing the E*Trade brand via several media outlets including print, online, and TV. The baby now has his own [YouTube Channel](http://www.youtube.com/etrade), [Facebook Fan Page](http://www.facebook.com/pages/ETRADE-Baby/45441344525), and [Twitter account](http://twitter.com/etradebaby). All of these only help to reaffirm why I think the [E*Trade](http://insidethatad.blogspot.com/www.etrade.com) "Talking Baby" campaign is one of the best currently out there and it is my current favorite. A link to the Adweek Media article can be found [here](http://www.adweek.com/aw/content_display/custom-reports/superbowl/e3i6e3ab16ed8b79d8bf4ea6c9239669cfe).

I read one comment on the baby's Facebook Fan Page where a particular fan states something to the effect of "I am a loyal E*Trade customer because of the baby." My only response to this was wow! But this is why I strongly believe that companies should show more respect towards their ad agencies and marketing departments. A memorable and humerous creative execution  will help bring and retain loyal customers.

E*Trade's lead ad agency is still [Grey](http://insidethatad.blogspot.com/www.greyglobalgroup.com) in New York.
