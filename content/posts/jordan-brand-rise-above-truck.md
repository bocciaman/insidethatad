This truck yields for no one! After [Nike Football’s MRAP-inspired promotional vehicle](http://www.freshnessmag.com/2010/05/15/nike-athletic-training-2010-media-showcase-nike-football-mrap-armored-vehicle/) and its [Transformer Mobile Retail Store](http://www.freshnessmag.com/2010/05/17/nike-athletic-training-2010-media-showcase-nike-football-transformer-mobile-retail-store/), [Jordan Brand](http://www.jumpman23.com/) will be introducing a new addition to its vehicle fleet – the Rise Above Truck. Covered in matte black motif along with an oversized Jumpman logo, the Truck is quipped with outdoor speakers and lights for those quick pick-up games. On board are also measuring instruments, including a vertical jump sensor, as well as countless pairs of Jordan Super.Fly.  The Jordan Brand – Rise Above Truck will be making stops at basketball venues cross country this summer. To follow its movement, simply sign up for Jordan Brand Twitter profile at [@jumpman23](http://twitter.com/jumpman23).
