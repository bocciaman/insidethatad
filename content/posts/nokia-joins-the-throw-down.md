So apparently, Nokia has decided to join the Apple bashing world when promoting its latest phone, the [Lumia 920](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lumia_920). Although, I have to say that the folks at Nokia weren't as clever as the folks over at Samsung at pointing out the shortcoming of the phone from Cupertino. I've discussed the Samsung ad in the [Samsung-Apple Throw Down](http://insidethatad.blogspot.com/2012/09/samsung-apple-throw-down.html). Nokia points out the lack of color options available to iPhone users. But even that isn't completely true because the iPhone is so popular that you can pretty much have the casing customized with whatever you want.
I have yet to find the agency behind this creative but for now, I am assuming it was done in-house. Because the spot is yet to air on TV.
