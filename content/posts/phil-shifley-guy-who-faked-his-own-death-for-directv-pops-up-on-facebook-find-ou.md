Not since they [sold Gregor the Russian billionaire's tiny giraffes](http://www.adweek.com/adfreak/tiny-giraffes-directv-ads-now-sale-126906) has DirecTV had a TV spot spin off amusingly into a random and goofy  online campaign. This time, Phil Shifley, the alias of the guy who faked  his own death and attended his own funeral in [the amusing DirecTV campaign from Grey,](http://www.adweek.com/news/advertising-branding/spot-death-cable-140810) has [popped up on Facebook.](http://www.facebook.com/PhilShifley) That's the extent of his known whereabouts—for now. DirecTV is challenging you to find him, and ["win bragging rights."](http://news.directv.com/2012/06/11/find-phil-shifley-win-bragging-rights/) That's not a great prize, but you can also just friend Phil and rap  with him. He's quite chatty, and needs all sorts of advice for a  "friend" who needs facial work and tips for surviving in the wilderness.
