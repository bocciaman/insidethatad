The leading technology in sandwich making, the Candwich, that’s right, it’s a sandwich in a can. The Candwich is made by Mark One Foods. This latest sandwich innovation comes in several flavors including: PB&J Candwich and BBQ Chicken Candwich, which are expected to hit the market in August. Their big claim to fame seems to be that they stay fresh for up to a year without needing any refrigeration hence the title. If Armageddon ever hit while I’m alive, I know what I’ll be having.
