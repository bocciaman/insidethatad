[![](/img/2017/10/Millennial-thumb.jpg)](/img/2017/10/Millennial-thumb.jpg)According to the [Pew Research Center](http://www.adrants.com/www.pewresearch.org/fact-tank/2016/04/25/millennials-overtake-baby-boomers/), Millennials are the nation's largest living generation. They are a age bracket that lack a long attention span; they crave experiences, activism, and constant connection. They are an age group that doesn't settle, which means your marketing initiatives have to be a bit more creative in order to attract the right attention.

If you're a business that wants to attract millennials, but haven't figured out how, these are some of the best tactics you can use to create a relevant relationship between your brand and Generation Y.
