German lingerie company Blush Berlin uses the Edward Snowden scandal to its marketing advantage by using playful promotional slogans including “Dear Edward Snowden, there’s still a lot to uncover,” and “Dear NSA, intimates—don’t spy them, buy them," [as Mashable originally](http://mashable.com/2013/07/01/edward-snowden-lingerie-ad/) reported regarding Blush's advertising campaign.

The Snowden ads are brought to you courtesy of Berlin-based [Glow](www.glow-berlin.de/‎).
