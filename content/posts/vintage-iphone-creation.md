To celebrate [WWDC 2012](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wwdc) that took place on Monday I wanted to share this vintage video of an iPhone mod-ing to vintage Apple rainbow colors. As most of you know, the WWDC is Apple’s developer conference where they sometimes introduce new products but mostly update current ones.
The iPhone mod, in the video above – really an aluminium back plate – will bring those memories rushing back to those of you who consider yourselves true “Cult of Mac” members.
I realize that that this post doesn’t discuss an ad, but it does discuss the logo and the brand itself – it is advertising.
