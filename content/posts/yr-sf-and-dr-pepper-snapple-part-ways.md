[Y&R](http://www.blogger.com/www.yr.com) and [Dr Pepper Snapple Group (DPSG)](http://www.blogger.com/www.drpeppersnapplegroup.com) will be parting ways after 40 years of working together according to an Ad Age article. In ’08 DPSG moved three of their brands away from Y&R to [Deutsch](http://www.blogger.com/www.deutschinc.com) , the three beverages moved are Dr Pepper, Snapple and Diet Dr Pepper. A shake up of brands happened again in February of 2010 when DPSG moved Canada Dry, Sunkist, and A&W Root Beer to [McGarryBowen](http://www.blogger.com/www.mcgarrybowen.com) without a review. This meant that Y&R only had 7UP after a 40-year relationship with DPSG.

So, why has changing ad agencies become so common in the industry today? Why is having an ad agency position so insecure?
